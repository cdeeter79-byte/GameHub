## 1. Introduction

Welcome to GameHub ("GameHub," "we," "us," or "our"). GameHub is a parent-directed youth sports aggregator application that allows parents and guardians to consolidate schedules, communications, and information from multiple youth sports platforms into a single, convenient experience.

This Privacy Policy describes how GameHub collects, uses, discloses, and retains personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). It also explains your rights with respect to your personal information and how to exercise those rights.

**This policy applies to:**
- Parents and guardians who create GameHub accounts
- Team managers and coaches who use GameHub's management features
- Child profiles created and managed by parents or guardians within a parent's account

**This policy does not apply to:**
- Third-party platforms, applications, or services that GameHub integrates with (such as TeamSnap, SportsEngine, or GameChanger), which are governed by their own privacy policies
- Any linked external websites or services not operated by GameHub

**Parent-Directed Platform:** GameHub is designed for and directed to adults — specifically parents, guardians, and team managers. Children do not independently create accounts or authenticate with GameHub. Child profiles are sub-accounts created and fully controlled by a parent or guardian. Please review Section 7 (Children's Privacy / COPPA) carefully.

By using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the Services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: information you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information obtained from third-party platforms you choose to connect.

### 2.1 Account and Registration Data

When you create a GameHub account, we collect:

- **Identity information:** Full name, email address, and password (stored in hashed form)
- **Account type:** Whether you are registering as a parent/guardian or as a team manager
- **Profile preferences:** Notification preferences, display settings, and language/locale settings
- **Verification data:** Where required, date of birth or age confirmation to verify that account holders are 13 years of age or older

### 2.2 Child Profile Data

Parents and guardians may create child profiles within their GameHub account. Child profiles are parent-controlled sub-accounts. For each child profile, we collect only the minimum information necessary:

- First name
- Last name (optional)
- Date of birth (used solely to calculate age-band groupings for team and schedule organization; not used for profiling or behavioral tracking)
- Sport(s) the child participates in
- Team assignments linked by the parent

Child profiles do not include an email address, phone number, password, or any independent login credentials. Children do not interact with GameHub directly. All data within a child profile is entered, managed, and controlled exclusively by the parent or guardian. See Section 7 (Children's Privacy / COPPA) and the Children's Privacy Addendum for additional detail.

### 2.3 Usage Data

When you use the Services, we automatically collect:

- **Interaction data:** Features accessed, screens viewed, actions taken within the app, and session duration
- **Search and filter activity:** Queries and filters applied within the schedule aggregation features
- **Error and diagnostic data:** Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics
- **Timestamps:** Dates and times of account activity

**Important:** Behavioral usage analytics are collected for adult account holders only (parents in their own account context, and managers). Usage data associated with child profile views is treated as parent-driven activity attributable to the parent's account and is not used for behavioral profiling or targeting.

### 2.4 Device and Technical Information

We collect certain technical information from your device, including:

- Device type, model, and operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (e.g., device ID)
- IP address
- Mobile network and connectivity information
- App version
- Push notification tokens (for sending schedule reminders and alerts)
- Time zone settings

### 2.5 Provider Integration Tokens and Credentials

GameHub integrates with a number of third-party youth sports platforms, including TeamSnap, SportsEngine, PlayMetrics, LeagueApps, GameChanger, BAND, Heja, Crossbar, ICS calendar feeds, and others. When you connect a provider account:

- We store OAuth tokens, API keys, or equivalent authentication credentials necessary to sync data on your behalf
- We retrieve and cache schedule data, team rosters (as shared by the provider), event information, and notifications from the connected platform
- These tokens are stored securely and used only to retrieve data that you have authorized us to access through the provider's authorization flow
- We do not store your passwords for third-party platforms
- Tokens are revocable by disconnecting the integration within the app or through the relevant provider's account settings

The data retrieved from provider platforms is governed by the provider's own terms of service and privacy policies, in addition to this policy.

### 2.6 Payment Information

GameHub offers subscription plans for parents (via RevenueCat) and manager plans (via Stripe). Payment processing is handled by these third-party processors:

- **RevenueCat:** Manages in-app purchase subscriptions on iOS and Android. GameHub receives subscription status, entitlement information, and anonymized purchase identifiers from RevenueCat. GameHub does not receive or store your full payment card details.
- **Stripe:** Processes payments for manager monthly and annual plans. Stripe receives and stores your payment card information. GameHub receives billing status, subscription metadata, and tokenized identifiers from Stripe.

For information about how RevenueCat and Stripe handle your payment data, please review their respective privacy policies.

We retain transaction records (subscription tier, billing status, dates) as necessary for account management and financial recordkeeping.

### 2.7 Communications Data

If you contact us for support, submit feedback, or communicate with us through in-app messaging or email, we collect the content of those communications and any contact information you provide.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Delivery and Operation

- Creating and maintaining your account
- Authenticating your identity when you log in
- Displaying consolidated schedules, team information, and event details pulled from connected provider platforms
- Syncing data with connected provider integrations on your behalf
- Enabling creation and management of child profiles within your account
- Processing subscription payments and managing your subscription status and entitlements
- Sending transactional communications (e.g., schedule reminders, sync error alerts, payment receipts)

### 3.2 Notifications and Alerts

- Sending push notifications for upcoming events, schedule changes, and team communications, based on your notification preferences
- Sending email notifications for account activity (e.g., new sign-in, subscription renewal, password changes)
- Delivering in-app alerts relevant to connected provider data

You may adjust your notification preferences in the app settings at any time.

### 3.3 Analytics and Service Improvement

- Analyzing how adult users interact with the Services to understand feature usage and improve the user experience
- Identifying bugs, errors, and performance issues
- Conducting internal research and analytics to develop new features and improvements
- **Analytics apply to adult account holders only.** Child profile data is excluded from behavioral analytics and usage profiling.

### 3.4 Advertising (Free Tier — Adult Users Only)

- Displaying contextually relevant advertising to adult users on the free subscription tier
- **Advertising is never served based on child profile data.** We do not use information about children's sports activities, teams, or schedules for advertising targeting.
- We do not engage in interest-based or behavioral advertising targeting based on child profile activity under any circumstances
- Premium subscribers may receive an ad-reduced or ad-free experience as described in the subscription benefits

### 3.5 Security and Fraud Prevention

- Detecting, investigating, and preventing unauthorized access, fraud, and other harmful activity
- Enforcing our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy
- Protecting the rights, safety, and property of GameHub, our users, and the public

### 3.6 Legal Compliance

- Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
- Responding to lawful requests from government authorities
- Maintaining records as required by law, including COPPA-related consent records

- Comply with a legal obligation, court order, or valid legal process

- Enforce our Terms of Service

### 4.4 Business Transfers

### 4.5 Aggregate and De-Identified Data

We may share aggregate, anonymized, or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you, for research, industry reporting, or marketing purposes.

### 4.6 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or your children's personal information to any third party for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, subject to the following:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Active account data | Retained while your account remains active |
| Child profile data | Retained until the child profile is deleted by the parent, or until the parent account is deleted |
| Provider integration tokens | Retained until you disconnect the integration or delete your account |
| Payment records | Retained as required by applicable financial and tax regulations (typically 7 years) |
| Deleted account data | 30-day grace period after account deletion request, then permanently purged from active systems |
| Backup systems | May retain data for up to 90 days in encrypted backup archives following deletion |
| Security and audit logs | Retained for 1 year from the date of the logged event |
| Support communications | Retained for up to 2 years from the date of the communication |

**Account Deletion Grace Period:** When you request account deletion, your account and associated data are flagged for deletion. You may reactivate your account within 30 days by logging in and canceling the deletion request. After 30 days, account data is permanently deleted from our active systems.

**Child Profile Deletion:** When a parent deletes a child profile, the profile data (name, date of birth, sport/team assignments) is permanently deleted. Event history associated with the child profile is anonymized or deleted within 30 days.

## 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This section is particularly important and applies to all child profiles created within GameHub.

### 7.1 Parent-Directed Model

GameHub is a parent-directed platform. The Services are designed for and directed to parents and guardians, not to children. Children do not create accounts, log in independently, or interact directly with GameHub. All child profiles are created, managed, and controlled exclusively by the parent or guardian account holder.

### 7.2 Age Requirements

You must be at least 13 years old to create a GameHub account. By creating an account, you represent that you are at least 13 years old. If you are a parent creating an account to manage your child's sports schedule, you represent that you are the parent or legal guardian of any child for whom you create a child profile.

### 7.3 Parental Consent for Child Profiles

By creating a child profile within your GameHub account, you — as the parent or guardian — provide consent for the collection and use of your child's information as described in this Privacy Policy and in the Children's Privacy Addendum. This consent is recorded automatically at the time the child profile is created.

### 7.4 Data Minimization for Child Profiles

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate child profiles:
- First name
- Last name (optional)
- Date of birth (for age-band grouping only)
- Sport(s)
- Team assignments

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, or independent credentials for child profiles. We do not engage in behavioral tracking of child profiles.

### 7.5 No Behavioral Tracking or Advertising Targeting

We do not use child profile data for behavioral analytics, interest-based advertising, or any form of profiling for commercial purposes. Advertising, if displayed, is never targeted based on child profile information.

### 7.6 Parental Rights Regarding Child Data

As the parent or guardian, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we have collected about your child by reviewing the child profile within your account or by submitting a request to us
- **Correct** inaccurate information by editing the child profile directly in the app

- **Refuse further collection** by deleting the child profile at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL] with your account email address and the name of the child profile. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days.

### 7.7 Reporting Concerns

If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child without proper parental consent, please contact us immediately at [PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL]. We will take prompt steps to delete such information.

For additional detail on how we handle child data, including COPPA verifiable parental consent procedures, please review the **Children's Privacy Addendum**, which supplements this Privacy Policy.

## 9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction, including:

- Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest within our Supabase database infrastructure
- Hashed storage of passwords using industry-standard algorithms
- Role-based access controls limiting employee access to personal data
- Secure token storage for provider integration credentials
- Regular security reviews and monitoring

No system is completely secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and applicable regulators as required by law.
