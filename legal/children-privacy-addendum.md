# GameHub — Children's Privacy Addendum

Last updated: 2026-04-11

---

## COPPA Parental Consent Disclosure

**This addendum is displayed to parents when they create a child profile in GameHub.**

---

## What We Collect

When you create a child profile in GameHub, we ask you to provide the following information about your child:

- **First name** (required) — Your child's given name or nickname
- **Sport(s)** (required) — The sport(s) your child plays (e.g., soccer, baseball, hockey)
- **Age band** (required) — Your child's age category: Under 13, Teen 13–17, or 18+
- **Avatar image** (optional) — A profile picture for identification

**We do NOT collect:**
- Exact date of birth
- Email address
- Phone number
- Home address
- School name
- Social media handles
- Health or medical information (including allergies)
- Geolocation (except when you manually request directions to an event)

---

## How We Use This Information

GameHub uses your child's information for these specific purposes only:

1. **Schedule aggregation** — Display your child's upcoming games, practices, and tournaments from all connected sports apps in one unified calendar
2. **RSVP management** — Allow you to respond to attendance requests on behalf of your child
3. **Team communication** — Deliver team messages and announcements relevant to your child's team(s)
4. **Account management** — Organize multiple children under your parent account
5. **Compliance** — Meet legal requirements for parental consent documentation

---

## Data Sharing with Third Parties

GameHub does NOT sell, rent, or lease your child's information to third parties.

**We share information only in these limited cases:**

| Third Party | Shares | Purpose | Your Control |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sports providers** (TeamSnap, SportsEngine, etc.) | Child name, sport, age band | Retrieve game/practice schedules; sync RSVP responses | You can disconnect a provider at any time |
| **Stripe** (payment processor) | No child data; only parent billing info | Process subscription payments | N/A |
| **RevenueCat** (subscription management) | No child data; only parent subscription status | Deliver premium features to parent account | N/A |
| **Supabase** (database host) | Encrypted child data at rest | Store and secure your data | Supabase is GDPR-compliant; located in EU/US |
| **Legal authorities** | Child data only if required by law | Respond to court order or legal process | Rare; you will be notified unless prohibited by law |

**We do NOT:**
- Share child data with advertisers
- Sell data to data brokers
- Allow analytics companies to track children under 13
- Use child data for profiling or marketing

---

## Parental Consent & Control

**By creating a child profile, you consent to:**
1. GameHub collecting, storing, and using your child's name, sport, and age band for the purposes listed above
2. Displaying your child's information only to you (the parent) and to verified team members and coaches
3. Using your child's age band to restrict ads and analytics (children under 13 see no ads; analytics disabled)

**Your control:**
- **View:** You can view all information we have about your child at any time in Settings → Children → [Child Name]
- **Edit:** You can edit your child's name, sport, or age band at any time
- **Delete:** You can delete your child's profile permanently; all associated data will be deleted after 30 days (you can undelete within 30 days)
- **Export:** You can export all your child's data as JSON in Settings → Privacy → Download My Data
- **Disconnect:** You can disconnect any sports provider and prevent future data syncs with that service

**Parental verification:**
We verify your identity as the account holder through:
- Google or Apple sign-in (you own the Google/Apple account used to sign up)
- Email confirmation (we send a verification email to your registered email address)
- Consent timestamp + IP address (logged for audit trail)

---

## Restricting Ads & Analytics for Children Under 13

**If you select "Under 13" as your child's age band, GameHub automatically:**

✅ **Disables:**
- Google Analytics events (we don't track analytics for this child)
- Ad SDK initialization (no ads are shown)
- Third-party marketing cookies
- Behavioral targeting data collection

✅ **Maintains:**
- Functional cookies (remember you're logged in)
- Essential security logging
- COPPA compliance audit logs (parent access only)

**For children 13–17:**
- Analytics are tracked with less granularity (no session recordings, no behavioral tracking)
- Ads are shown but NOT personalized (context-based only: "sports app" category)
- No direct marketing emails sent to the child

**For adults 18+:**
- Full personalized ads and analytics
- Direct marketing emails allowed

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

### Normal Use
Your child's data is stored as long as the profile is active. Once you delete the profile:
- **Days 0–30:** Soft delete — data is hidden from the UI but retained. You can restore the profile.
- **Days 30+:** Hard delete — data is permanently removed from our database.

### Account Closure
If you delete your parent account:
- All child profiles are deleted
- All RSVP records, messages, and audit logs are deleted
- 90-day retention period for legal/tax compliance, then permanent deletion

### Legal Requirements
We may retain some data if required by law (e.g., tax, legal hold). We will notify you if this occurs.

## Security Measures

**We protect your child's data with:**
- **Encryption in transit:** All data sent to GameHub is encrypted with HTTPS
- **Encryption at rest:** Child data is encrypted in our database
- **Access controls:** Only you (and verified team members/coaches with explicit permission) can access your child's information
- **Row-Level Security:** Database policies prevent cross-family data access
- **No third-party access:** Sports providers cannot see children from other families
- **Audit logging:** All access to your child's data is logged for security review

**Data breach response:**
If we discover unauthorized access to child data, we will:
1. Immediately investigate and contain the breach
2. Notify you within 48 hours
3. Comply with COPPA breach notification requirements
4. Provide credit monitoring (if applicable)
